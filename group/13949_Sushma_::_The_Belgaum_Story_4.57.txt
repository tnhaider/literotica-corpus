 1981 Sushma in Belgaum

An experienced but unsatisfied woman fulfills and energizes me

Synopsis

This is a depiction of a true episode I had during my vacation. I had encountered this lady, who was but a few years older than I. While swotting during my long vacation, she was a welcome relief in my work time. I have cut short a lot of repetition, and tried to retain the essence of the relation. 

There is descriptive sex, but it is placed in context, giving a priority to the story.

I guess Literotica readers can understand. Certain italicized words can be understood from the context and need no translation.
